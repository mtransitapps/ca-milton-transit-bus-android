Full description:
This app adds Milton Transit buses information to MonTransit.

This app provides the buses schedule and news from @miltontransit on Twitter.

Milton Transit buses serve Milton in Ontario, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by the Town of Milton.
http://icreateopendata.public.esolutionsgroup.ca/home/details/9765286c-48b4-417c-8c23-3a64334e6c04

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-milton-transit-bus-android

This app is not related with Milton Transit and the Town of Milton.

Permissions:
